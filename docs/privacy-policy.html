Privacy Policy — Sama (Simple Meditation Timer)

No Data Collection

Sama does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information of any kind.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All information you create in the app — sessions, journal entries, presets, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app contains no analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any third-party tracking code. We have no way of knowing how you use the app, and we don't want to.

No Internet Connection Required

Sama works entirely offline. It makes no network requests of any kind. No internet connection is needed to use any feature of the app.

No User Accounts

The app does not require or offer user accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.
